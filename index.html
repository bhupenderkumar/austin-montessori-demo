Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for 3- and 4-year-olds, providing a nurturing environment that encourages exploration and discovery. Our experienced teachers use the Montessori method to foster a love of learning and prepare students for future success.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students engage in hands-on learning experiences that build on their natural curiosity and love of discovery. Our experienced teachers provide personalized attention and support to help students thrive academically and socially.

🚀

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs are designed to challenge and engage students as they grow and mature. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching methods to help students develop strong academic skills and a love of learning.
